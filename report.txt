1ï¸âƒ£ Project Overview

EcoReceipt is a sustainability-focused receipt analysis system that extracts product information from uploaded receipt images and evaluates their environmental impact.

The system performs:

OCR-based text extraction

Product name cleaning and parsing

Fuzzy matching against a sustainability database

Environmental impact scoring

Alternative product recommendation

Dashboard visualization

PDF improvement guide generation

The goal of Phase 1 was to build a structured, modular, scalable system capable of evolving into a production-grade sustainability intelligence platform.

Consumers are often unaware of the environmental impact of their daily purchases.

Receipts contain valuable behavioral data, but:

Product names vary

Impact information is not directly available

This project solves:

Automatic receipt understanding

Sustainable product scoring

Actionable eco-friendly suggestions
